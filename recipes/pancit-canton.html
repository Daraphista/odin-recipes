Pancit Canton

Description

Pancit Canton is a very popular food among Pinoys. It is an instant-cook noodle dish with flavorful condiments. It is typically contained in a plastic bag with dry noodles and the condiments in saches alongside the noodles. It is an easy dish to prepare and that makes it a super convenient meal that can be prepared any time of the day.

Ingredients

When you buy pancit canton, it will already contain most of the ingredients you will need!

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Powdered seasoning
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil 1 and a half cups of water in a pot.
  2. When water reaches boiling point, put in the pasta. No salt or oil needed.
  3. Check the softness of the noodles with a fork. To get the perfect texture, take it out just before it is soft enough. Don't worry, the pasta is already ready before you feel like it's ready. Wait a little longer, and your noodles will be overcooked.
  4. Strain the water and put the noodles on a plate. Add the condiments and seasoning and mix well.
  5. It is now ready to serve!
