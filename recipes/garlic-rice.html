Garlic Rice

Description

Rice is a staple of the Philippines, as it is in many Asian countries. It is the mainstay of every meal, be it breakfast, lunch or dinner. Garlic rice specifically became the go-to option in the island country for the flavor and smells it brought to the table.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of day old rice
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of cooking oil

Steps

  1. Mince the 3 cloves of garlic.
  2. Heat the pan with oil.
  3. Once the pan is heated,saute the garlic until crispy and brown and set aside
  4. When garlic is set aside, begin frying rice until they aren't sticky and moist.
  5. Serve with the brown crispy garlic as it's toppings. You can also serve it with chopped spring onions as it's toppings
